Regulatory Compliance Framework

UAE-Based Security Token Platform with Lebanese Asset Access

Jurisdictional Strategy

AuraKey Capital employs a sophisticated dual-jurisdictional approach that maximizes regulatory clarity while maintaining access to target assets. The structure is designed for full compliance with both UAE financial regulations and Lebanese property laws.

UAE Operating Entity

  • Regulator: VARA (Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority)
  • License: Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP)
  • Token Classification: Security Token / Asset-Referenced Virtual Asset
  • Key Advantage: Clear digital securities framework

Lebanese Asset Structure

  • Vehicle: Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)
  • Governance: Lebanese Corporate Law
  • Asset Holding: Direct property title ownership
  • Key Advantage: Local legal compliance and asset protection

Security Token Compliance

Our tokens are structured as regulated digital securities, providing investors with legal protection and institutional-grade compliance standards.

Legal Classification

Tokens represent ownership in Lebanese SPVs, classified as securities under UAE law

Investor Protection

Full transparency, regular reporting, and legal recourse for token holders

Regulatory Reporting

Automated compliance with KYC, AML, and transaction reporting requirements
